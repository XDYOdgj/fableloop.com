Citrisurf 2310: The Ultimate Stainless Steel Rust Remover

Citrisurf 2310 is a powerful, gel-based rust remover and passivation cleaner that works in both commercial and residential settings. Its concentrated formula blends industrial-grade citric acid to tackle even the toughest rust and oxidation stains, all while preserving the integrity of stainless steel surfaces.

Designed with a high viscosity gel formulation, Citrisurf 2310 sticks to vertical surfaces and railings with ease, making it ideal for cleaning hard-to-reach areas. Unlike traditional sprays, this gel ensures even application and consistent results without dripping or runoff.

What sets Citrisurf 2310 apart is its ability to not only remove rust but also prevent it from coming back. By eliminating free iron, it helps maintain a clean, corrosion-resistant surface that stays looking great for longer. This makes it a perfect choice for high-stakes environments like medical and food facilities, where safety and hygiene are top priorities.

With compliance to NSF, ASTM, and AMS standards, you can trust that this US-made product is safe and effective for all applications. Each 22-ounce bottle covers up to 45 square feet, offering a cost-efficient solution for keeping your metal surfaces pristine and rust-free.
