Method Daily Wood Cleaner

Method Daily Wood Cleaner is a powerful, plant-based cleaner crafted to revive the natural beauty of your wooden furniture. Free from harsh chemicals, it's designed for daily use without compromising the integrity of your wood. This 28 oz spray bottle delivers a fresh, clean finish using advanced green chemistry, which effectively removes dust, grime, and even stubborn stains. Its gentle yet effective formula ensures that your furniture retains its original grain and sheen while being protected from wear and tear. With a delightful almond scent, it makes cleaning a pleasant experience, and its non-toxic ingredients make it safe for households with children and pets. Plus, the recyclable packaging supports a greener planet. Choose Method for reliable, sustainable wood care that works as hard for your home as it does for the environment.
