The Estes 7263 Hex-3 Flying Rocket Kit

Bring the thrill of space exploration to your living room with this delightful flying model rocket kit. Crafted with precision and quality, it's designed to inspire creativity and curiosity in model builders of all ages. Whether you're a family enthusiast, a science fair participant, or simply looking for a fun hands-on activity, the Estes 7263 is the perfect choice.

Each piece is thoughtfully designed to ensure an enjoyable and educational experience. The durable high-quality Estes body tubes provide a strong structure, while the laser-cut wood fins add intricate detail to every corner of the model. With the molded plastic nose cone and preassembled parachute already included, you can focus on painting and customizing your rocket to make it truly unique. The easy-to-apply water-slide decals allow for creative personalization without the hassle of complicated application techniques.

Once built and painted, this rocket is ready for an exciting flight that can reach up to 900 feet with the C6-5 engine. It's not just a model; it's a comprehensive way to learn about the science and art of rocketry, making it an ideal project for students and hobbyists alike.

Key Features

  • High-quality Estes body tubes for durability and strength
  • Laser-cut wood fins for precise constructions
  • Colorful preassembled parachute for safe recovery
  • Easy-to-apply water-slide decals for detailed customization
  • Corkscrew flight capability up to 900 feet with C6-5 engine

Perfect For

This model rocket kit is ideal for ages 10 and up. Younger builders (under 12) should be supervised by an adult to ensure safety and proper assembly techniques. It's great for family projects, school science fair entries, or simply a rewarding DIY activity.

Why Choose Estes?

Estes is a trusted brand in model rocketry, known for its reliable performance and high-quality kits. The Estes 7263 Hex-3 is no exception, offering a blend of fun, learning, and creativity that makes every build a memorable experience. With its easy-to-follow instructions and engaging design, it's a fantastic way to introduce young minds to the wonders of aerospace engineering.
