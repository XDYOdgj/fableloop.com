Roebic K-97 Drain Cleaner

Your go-to solution for clean, healthy drainage in both residential and commercial settings. This advanced drain cleaner is specially formulated to tackle tough clogs and maintain the efficiency of your plumbing system without the use of harmful chemicals.

Key Features

  • Efficiently clears main line clogs and sewage buildup with fast-acting bacteria enzymes
  • Safe for use in all types of plumbing, including toilets and sewer lines
  • Perfect for low flow toilets to prevent sludge accumulation and keep drains running smoothly
  • Easy one-step application with simple flushing instructions for hassle-free use
  • Helps break down waste in septic tanks, making it ideal for families needing regular maintenance

Why Choose Roebic K-97?

Whether you're dealing with recurring blockages or simply looking to prevent them, Roebic K-97 delivers reliable performance. Its eco-friendly formula is not only safe for your home but also gentle on the environment. By using natural bacteria enzymes, it enhances the cleanliness of your system while keeping your pipes free from grease and other stubborn deposits. It’s a must-have for any home, new or old, that values efficient, sustainable drainage solutions.

How to Use

Apply Roebic K-97 with just one simple step. Simply pour it down the drain and flush with hot water. For long-term effectiveness, repeat the process every six months to maintain optimal flow and prevent future buildup. Trust in a cleaner, greener way to care for your drains and plumbing.
