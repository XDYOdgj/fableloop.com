Activa Blackjack White Earthenware Clay

Whether you're a seasoned potter or a hobbyist with a passion for crafting, Activa Blackjack White Earthenware Clay offers an unmatched blend of quality and usability. Its silky-smooth texture makes shaping and molding effortless, allowing for precise detailing and beautifully intricate designs.

Known for its exceptional plasticity, this clay holds its form well during the creative process, making it perfect for both functional and artistic projects. Designed with educational use in mind, it's a reliable and forgiving material that helps students achieve professional results without frustration.

The Activa Blackjack clay has a moisture content of 16-20%, which ensures it's easy to handle and mix. Fired to Cone 06-04, it produces a dense, non-porous finish that’s ready for immediate use. This versatile earthenware clay is ideal for a wide range of applications including flower pots, decorative sculptures, and arts and crafts.

Key Features

  • Professional-quality, low-fire clay suitable for Cone 06-04 firing
  • Smooth texture for effortless shaping and molding
  • High plasticity ideal for pot throwing, hand building, and sculpture
  • Light grey when wet, fires to a smooth white talc body
  • Excellent glaze compatibility for both greenware and bisqueware

Its natural composition and reliable performance make it a favorite in both personal and classroom settings. Whether you're looking to make flower pots, sculptures, or decorative pieces, this clay delivers consistent quality that’s easy to work with and reliable in results.
